About The Bay Point

A place where delicious food, memorable celebrations and genuine hospitality come together.

Our Story

Located along Salama Road in Homa Bay Town, opposite 112 Tavern and adjacent to Abila Kamwalimu Hotel, The Bay Point was established to provide residents and visitors with a modern dining experience centered around quality food and outstanding customer service.

From our signature Nyama Choma to freshly prepared fish dishes, delicious chicken meals, local favourites, tropical juices and celebration cakes, every meal is prepared with passion using fresh ingredients.

Whether you are meeting friends, celebrating life's milestones or enjoying a family meal, our restaurant provides a comfortable atmosphere that makes every visit memorable.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dining experiences through delicious meals, warm hospitality and excellent customer service.

Our Vision

To become the most preferred restaurant and celebration destination in Homa Bay County.

Our Values

Quality, Integrity, Freshness, Hospitality, Innovation and Customer Satisfaction.
